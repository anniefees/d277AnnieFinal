History

The earliest known inhabitants are the Ute and Shashone Native American tribes. The city was founded in 1847 by Brigham Young and approximately 150 Mormons fleeing from religious prosecution.

Today

Today, the city is still home to the headquarters of the Mormon church. Approximately 49% of the population is Mormon. Salt lake city has a host of oppurtunities. It is home to world class arts, culture, dinning, and most famously winter sports. Hundreds of thousands of people flock to Salt Lake City every year to experience the illustrious natural beauty that can be found in the surrounding mountains and valleys. The Great Salt Lake is another natural phenomenon that attracts many tourists yearly. It is one of the few lakes that can be seen from outerspace.
