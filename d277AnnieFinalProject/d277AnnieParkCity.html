This popular ski town was settled by Parley P. Pratt in 1884. The city later became a popular spot for silver, gold, and lead mining. Today is is well known for many ski, hiking, and mountain biking opportunites, as well as, the annual Sundance film festival.

  2. Park City Village - Park City Mountain Resort
  3. Deer Valley
  4. Woodward
